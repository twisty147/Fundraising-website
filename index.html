About Us

Our mission is to promote inclusivity, empowerment, and personal well-being through the sport of para table tennis for individuals with disabilities.

  • High-quality coaching and training for athletes.
  • Increase participation and awareness within the disability community.
  • Organize tournaments at all levels.
  • Foster an inclusive community.
